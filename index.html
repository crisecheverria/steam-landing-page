About

We are a platform that aims to connect women in the fields of STEM. This is done by connecting women to companies that are working towards a more diverse environment; connecting women to mentors/ coaches in these fields that can be a reference and example to empower them to excel.

Connecting women to their dream company

By promoting companies who are working towards having an inclusive and diverse culture. Imagine you find your dream company and on top of that, you find out that they are all about empowering women and giving them the right tools to succeed. This space is designed to connect you to companies around the world that are championing and working towards inclusion and diversity in STEM.

Connecting women to mentors in the industry

By connecting women to experts/ coaches on the fields, in order to give them guidance in their journey. Imagine you found the dream job but you do not know how to get there yet. Imagine finding that power woman that can take you along the journey to show you how she/he has done it, perfect match for a win situation.

Connecting to like minded women

Maybe you want to know how other project managers work in other companies. We connect women in STEM with like minded women. Do you feel alone in your field? Are you the only women studying or working in STEM? We are here to find your best mate, that person that is at your same level and can be a support in your journey. Who knows what the future can bring when you can find a person that has the same interest.

We promote all STEM events that empower women

We promote all STEM events that empower women, inclusion and diversity around the world. We want you to be informed and equipped to continue your journey. These events are perfect for you to connect with others but also to grow in your knowledge.

STEMA was created in order to help close the gender GAP in the areas of technology, science, engineering and areas where women are underrepresented. We want to empower women to believe they can also fulfil their destiny and calling in these fields. We are here to empower the next generation of women in STEM.
